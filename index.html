Rimuru Tempest

Rimuru Tempest , formerly known as Satoru Mikami, is the main protagonist of That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime.
Partner and best friend of the True Dragon Veldora Tempest, Rimuru is the founder and King of the Jura Tempest Federation in Jura Forest. He is one of the Demon Lords of the Octagram. He lives in Rimuru City, the Capital city of the Jura Tempest Federation.
Rimuru initially appears as a blue-bodied Slime, but upon becoming a Demon Slime, his body turns silver with streaks of gold.
Upon becoming a True Demon Lord and evolving his race from Slime to Demon Slime, Rimuru has been depicted wearing a black overcoat. His human form appears slightly older.
He has a kind heart and a quirky personality. Despite his power and abilities, Rimuru's greatest asset is his cunning and shrewd decision-making. Rimuru has survived against dangerous opponents such as Milim Nava and Hinata Sakaguchi,ssimply by outsmarting them. Rimuru prefers diplomatic solutions and will avoid conflict whenever possible, but isn't afraid to wage war when necessary, as shown when the Kingdom of Falmuth invaded.
